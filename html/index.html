THE PROBLEM

Plastic solved many of our daily problems when it was invented. We could store items in containers now, in our desired sizes, water in bottles, food in containers, trash in plastic bags. For years we used plastic with no apparent consequence.

However, those times have changed. Now we know the harm that is brought upon oceans due to our excessive use of plastic. It is no longer ok not to know about the problem.

Our problem now is not to single handedly clean up the ocean but make sure that all of us know about the sustainable goals that companies can follow. Help us let the world know that being sustainable isnt a practice that should be followed but that needs to be followed before anything else
